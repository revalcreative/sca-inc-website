PWCA SUPPLEMENTAL UNEMPLOYMENT BENEFIT PLAN

The PWCA Supplemental Unemployment Benefit Plan, or SUB Plan, uses fringe benefit dollars to provide income replacement for employees who have involuntary work stoppages.

Employers that provide the PWCA SUB Plan to their employees eliminate the additional payroll tax burden of paying fringe dollars in cash.

WHY CHOOSE THE SUB PLAN?

PROTECT YOUR EMPLOYEES. IMPROVE YOUR BOTTOM LINE.

The PWCA Supplemental Unemployment Benefit (SUB) Plan allows employers to use fringe benefit dollars to fund tax-free income replacement for employees who experience involuntary job loss between government contracts or seasonal work.

With the SUB Plan:

  • Employees receive weekly benefits when out of work through no fault of their own
  • Employers reduce the need to pay out taxable fringe dollars as cash
  • You stay compliant with wage determinations under the Service Contract Act

HOW IT WORKS?

SIMPLE TO SET UP. EASY TO ADMINISTER.

The SUB Plan is a qualified benefit program under IRS guidelines. Contributions are made pre-tax, and employees receive tax-free benefits.

SCA, Inc handles all the administration ‚Äî from compliance to claims management.

The process is simple:

  1. Employer allocates fringe dollars to the SUB Plan.
  2. SCA, Inc. manages the funds in a secure trust account.
  3. Employees who are laid off or between contracts can file a claim.
  4. Eligible employees receive weekly benefit payments tax-free.
